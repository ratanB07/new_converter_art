Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI Anime Generator

How does the AI anime generator work?

Our AI anime generator uses state-of-the-art machine learning models trained on millions of anime artworks. The system analyzes your text description and style preferences to generate unique artwork that matches your vision. The more detailed your description, the better the results will be.

Do I own the rights to the generated artwork?

Yes! With all our paid plans, you own full commercial rights to the artwork you generate. Our free tier is for personal use only. You can use your generated artwork for merchandise, games, books, and other commercial projects without any restrictions.

How long does it take to generate an artwork?

Generation time depends on the resolution and complexity of your request. Most artworks generate in 10-30 seconds. Higher resolution images (4K-8K) may take up to 1-2 minutes. Our Pro users enjoy priority generation with faster speeds and dedicated server resources.

Can I edit an artwork after it's generated?

Absolutely! You can make adjustments to your prompt and generate new variations. We also provide an advanced editor where you can make manual adjustments to colors, proportions, and other details after generation. Our Professional plan includes Photoshop-style editing tools.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! New users get 5 free generations to test the platform. No credit card required. After your free trial, you can choose a subscription plan that fits your needs. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.
